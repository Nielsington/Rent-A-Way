Consoles

Do you miss the good old days when you spend your days playing videogames on your console?

You're not the only one. We at Rent-A-Way felt the same way.



We as humans love looking back to the 'old school times'. In the last few decades everything related to tech got a huge improvement: phones, TV's, computers, but this is also the case for consoles. Everyone has these moments where they look back to how certain products were before, and how we used them back then. We at Rent-A-Way strive to bringing back these nostalgic feelings to everyone.


Buying old consoles is often too expensive for the amount of time you're going to play on them. That's why we provide rentals for different consoles from different brands from different times. We provide the option to rent weekly, monthly and yearly so we can fit everyones needs. All our consoles get a maintenance and deep cleaning after use. This way our clients always get a clean and 'up-to-date' console. Below you can find all consoles we offer:
